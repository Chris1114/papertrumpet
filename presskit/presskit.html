Songsmith John Dyer is a progressive vocalist, guitarist and lyricist whose works are spoken of as "luscious", hypnotic", "sweetly flowing", "thoughtful and engaging", and "fresh and unpredictable". Nurtured by folk, rock, jazz, pop, visual and literary influences, with heroes such as Bob Dylan, Lou Reed, Neil Young, Kahlil Gibran, Paul Klee, Charlie Chaplin, and John Coltrane, Dyer's meditations and tales of self discovery are at once unique yet accessible. Founder of Texas based band Drama Tiki (1992-1998) who independently released three albums and were voted Best Folk/Acoustic Group 1998 by the Fort Worth Weekly, Dyer now appears in the flesh with acoustic and song in Chapel Hill, NC and surrounding areas. Dyer was recently honored as a finalist in the NCSC Songwriters contest 2000 and has just released his debut solo album One Among, a mosaic of spirited melody, cathartic rhythm, and playful and explosive vocalizations. Experience Dyer's character, drive, and vision directly, nerves exposed, bares bones, truly, intimately.
