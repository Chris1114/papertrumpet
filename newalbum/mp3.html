MP3's are the first really viable way of getting great sounding music over the internet. Don't expect them to start playing immediately, though. They take some time, but it's way less time than the comparable sounding original CD tracks. So feel free to keep looking around the site...the download should keep going in the background. Once you have downloaded the MP3, chances are you will want to listen to it. To do this, you will need a program called an MP3 player. There are many to choose from, and newer computers come with players already installed. If you need to get a player, Apple Computer makes a great one called Quicktime that plays many different types of media, including MP3's. Click here to check it out.
